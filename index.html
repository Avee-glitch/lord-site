UNDERWORLD LAB

Hidden risks and unintended consequences of AI systems.

Underworld Lab explores how AI systems behave outside ideal conditions — when assumptions fail, incentives misalign, and edge cases dominate.

This is not a showcase of polished demos. It is a collection of case files, observations, and failures from constrained and non-ideal environments.

Focus

  • AI failure modes and silent breakdowns
  • Unintended behavior under constraints
  • Systems that technically “work” but fail in practice
  • Hidden risks beneath clean demos

What This Is Not

  • Tutorials
  • Marketing content
  • Product demos
  • Hype-driven AI takes

Most AI systems fail quietly.
Understanding those failures matters more than celebrating success.

Case files are added as systems break.
